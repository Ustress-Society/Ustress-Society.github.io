Stressor kit

Ustress offers you an amazing experience by combining stress, productivity and community. If you are ambitious and you want to achieve all your goals, join our elite community and buy our kit. It consists of:

Tower

Designed with brutalist aesthetic, the tower will spread the noise all over the room thanks to its speakers.

Badge

A must-wear to show your membership, during the session it will detect the data and turn them into a stress level.

How

Ustress gives you a device that through the production of different noises, it will increase the productivity of the group during working hours. It will be similar to a personal challenge against others in order to endure noise.
The data will be collected and turned into information through the online platform. In this way the user can show and share his Stress level and his membership to the Ustress community.

STEP 1: THE TEAM

The kit requires a team of employee.
The group must use the badges to collect data during the work session.

STEP 2: POWER ON

Turn on the tower from the power button on its back.
Touch the eye with your badge to activate it.

STEP 3: START SESSION

The tower will steadily increase the noise enhancing stress and group productivity.
The badge will detect the data.

STEP 4: TOWER BREAK

To stop the noise you can hold the red button after scanning your badge.
When the button is released the tower will restart.

STEP 5: END SESSION

Turn off the tower from the power button. The badge will be disabled too.
Show with pride your stress with our badge.

STEP 6: STRESS LEVEL

The data collected defines your Stress Level.
This score will put your team on a leaderboard. The higher the position, the better the rewards!

Choose your kit

Each kit contains a Tower, 5 or 10 badges (depending which one you choose) and a user manual that explain how our device works.
